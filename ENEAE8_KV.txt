A Prayer to Jamyang Khyentse Wangpo Single embodiment of the wisdom, love, and spiritual power Of all the victorious ones and their heirs, Mañjughoṣa and the rest, Glorious guru, lord among learned and accomplished masters, To you I pray-look upon me with eyes of compassion! Pacify the sufferings of this life, the next, and the bardo state! Increase longevity, merit, prosperity, favorable circumstances, and everything positive! And may all beings connected with you Through your blessings attain the level of enlightenment in the pure land of Sukhāvatī! Khyentse Wangpo himself wrote this prayer in response to the entreaties of the faithful. Virtue!
